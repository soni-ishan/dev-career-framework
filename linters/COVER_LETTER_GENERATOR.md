The goal is NOT to repeat your resume. The goal is to prove you are a human who actually read the job description.

## The Formula: `[Hook]` + `[Proof]` + `[Close]`

---

### Phase 1: The "Hook"
*Do not start with "I am writing to apply..."*

"I first discovered [Company Name] when I [Personal Story/Interaction with Product or Mission]. I was immediately drawn to your mission to [Company Mission stated in their own words], because it aligns with my drive to [Your Personal Goal/Value]. I am excited to apply for the [Job Title] role because it sits at the perfect intersection of my experience in [Skill Area A] and my passion for [Industry/Tech Domain]."

### Phase 2: The "Body" (The API Connection)
*Connect your "Source Code" (Skills) to their "Docs" (Job Requirements).*

"In your job description, you mention a need for [Specific Problem/Requirement from JD]. During my time as [Role/Student], I tackled a similar challenge when I [Context of Project]. I utilized [Hard Skill/Tool] to [Action], which resulted in [Result/Metric]. I am confident I can bring this same rigour to [Company Name] to help your team [Specific Team Goal found in news/JD]."

"Additionally, I see you are looking for experience with [Key Tech Stack Requirement]. I have architected [Project Name] using [Tech Stack], where I specifically learned to manage [Technical Conflict/Constraint]. This technical foundation allows me to hit the ground running with your engineering team."

### Phase 3: The "Close"
*Confidenct and concise.*

"I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my background in [Skill A] and [Skill B] can contribute to the [Team Name] team. Thank you for your time and consideration."
