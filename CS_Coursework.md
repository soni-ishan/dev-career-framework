=========================

**How to use this:**

1.  Find the course you have completed.

2.  **Choose ONE "Angle"** that best matches what you actually did or enjoyed in that class. Do not list all three.

3.  **Fill in the `[brackets]`**. You **MUST** replace the bracketed text with the specific details of your assignment or project.

4.  Copy the final bullet into your "Projects" or "Education" section on your resume.
